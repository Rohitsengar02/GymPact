Bicycle crunchs

Steps For Doing Bicycle crunchs

1. First lie on your back with your knees bend. Feet should flat on the ground as wide as your hips.
2. With your hands behind your head and your Elbows bent out sides, use your abs to lift your sholder blade off the ground.
3. At the same time, bring your right knee to meet your left elbows.
4. When the right knee is bent, straight your left leg and reach it out in front of you at a 45 degree angle.
5. Perform on the opposite side , bringing your left knee to your right elbows, straightening the right leg.
6. Continue alternating legs and squeeze your core. Brush your inner things together as you switch sides to ensure you are hugging your legs in towards the midline of your body.
