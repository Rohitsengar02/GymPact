Chest Workouts

Chest exercies basically works on the pectoral muscles, the large fan-shaped muscles on the front of the chest. Chest exercies can be broken into two basic motions.They can be press and fly.

The brench press is a classic exercies for chest that utilize the pressing motion.
The dumbell fly is a classic chest exercies that utilize the fly motion.

Benifits:-

1. Chest exercies working on the pectoral muscles and it also prevent breast sagging.
2.It also helps to support deeper breadthing because the pecs are attached to your ribs. which expand with every breadth.
3. working the chest muscles has the added benefits of toning and strengthening the muscles in your arms and upper back.

Some popular chest exercies are:-
1. Push-ups
2. Dips
3. Inclined Dumbell Flys
4. Barbell brench press
5. Chest press machine
6. Cable Cross overs
